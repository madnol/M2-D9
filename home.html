Philosophy of Miracle

Philosophy of Miracle is the debut album by Russian singer Vitas, released in 2001. It is sometimes translated as Philosophy of a Miracle, Philosophy of Wonder, or Wonders of Philosophy. The album received widespread popularity in mid-2011 after "The 7th Element" and "Opera 2" gained popularity several years earlier.

Band

DIVA (ДИВА) is the name of the band that accompanies Vitas during his concerts.
